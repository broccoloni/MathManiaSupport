How Math Mania works

Math Mania is an endless set of number and logic riddles. Each level shows a puzzle with exactly one missing value (a “?”). Your goal is to find the missing number.

Levels are generated deterministically from two numbers: your level and your World Seed. That means: the same World Seed + the same level always gives the same puzzle. If you want a totally different run of puzzles, change your World Seed in the app’s Settings.

Tip: If you’re reporting a bug, include the level number and world seed.
