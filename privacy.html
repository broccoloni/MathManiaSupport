Privacy Policy

Summary

Math Mania is designed to work without creating an account. The app stores your progress (like your current level and settings) locally on your device.

Ads

If you enable ads in your build, the app may display ads provided by third-party ad networks (for example, Google AdMob). These networks may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads and measure performance.

Update this section to match the ad/analytics SDKs you actually use.

What we collect

  • On-device game data: level progress, settings (including World Seed), and basic stats. Stored locally.
  • Support messages (if you contact us): the email address you provide and the contents of your message.
  • Ad/diagnostics data (if enabled): identifiers and events required by ad SDKs and crash reporting.

How we use information

  • To operate the app and keep your progress.
  • To respond to support requests.
  • To show ads (if enabled) and improve stability.
