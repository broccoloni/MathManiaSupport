Overview

Math Mania (“the App”) is an offline-first puzzle game. You can play without creating an account. Your progress (such as your current level, world seed, and basic stats) is stored locally on your device.

Information we collect

  • On-device game data (stored locally): level progress, world seed/settings, and gameplay stats (for example: solved/skipped counts and solve times).
  • Support messages (if you contact us): information you choose to provide through the Support page form (for example: your message and an email address if you enter one).
  • Advertising data (via Google AdMob): if ads are enabled, the App uses the Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob). Google (and its partners) may collect information to show ads, limit fraud, and measure ad performance.

Ads (Google AdMob)

The App may show ads provided by Google AdMob. When ads are shown, Google may automatically collect certain information, which can include:

  • Device identifiers (such as the Advertising ID on Android, and similar identifiers on iOS).
  • Device / app information (e.g., device type, OS version, app version, language, and region).
  • IP address (often used to estimate general location such as city/region).
  • Ad interaction data (e.g., impressions, clicks, time of interaction, and basic diagnostics).

Google may use this information to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, depending on your device settings, region, consent choices (where applicable), and the App’s ad configuration.

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy · AdMob & data · Google partner sites

How we use information

  • To run the App and keep your progress/settings on your device.
  • To respond to support requests you send through the Support page.
  • To show ads (if enabled) and measure performance and reliability through the ad provider.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as described below:

  • AdMob (Google): if ads are enabled, data may be collected and processed by Google as described above.
  • Support provider: if you use the Support contact form, your message is processed by the form service (and delivered to us by email). The form service and email provider will process your submission to deliver it.

Data storage and retention

  • Game data is stored locally on your device and remains until you uninstall the App or clear the App’s storage/data.
  • Support messages are retained as long as needed to respond and maintain support history, and may be deleted periodically.
  • Ad-related data is handled by Google under their retention practices (see links above).

Your choices

  • Reset advertising ID: on many devices you can reset or limit the Advertising ID in system settings.
  • Ad personalization: depending on your region, you may be shown consent options for personalized ads, or you can adjust ad personalization in your Google/Apple settings.
  • Support form: you can choose what information to include when contacting support.

Children’s privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). If you believe a child has provided personal information through the Support form, please contact us and we’ll delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the latest version.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, please use the contact form on the Support page.
